Grizzly Game Day

The Montana Griz Softball team in just it's 3rd year of existence, has built quite the reputation as a program that provides the full game-day experience.

National Anthem

Baseball is America's Past time, the two things simply go together. Standing and removing our caps to honor our troops with the playing of our national athem signals the beginning of the game at Grizzly softball complex, or as we like to call it"The Den"

Food

Nothing beats ball-park food: Nachos, sloppy hot-dogs, iced-cold coke, cracker jacks and snickers bars are all enjoyed at grizzly Softball complex.

Ball Park music!

We always play tunes at the field, it motivates the athletes and entertains the fans! Often times Monte even dances along.
